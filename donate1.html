Friends of Chiara Home

As more than 1 in 5 children have one or more emotional, behavioral, development or physical disability, it is clear that many families care for persons in their home with special needs . You probable know many families like this yourself. Chiara Home provides temporary out-of-home care to people of all ages with special needs. While caregivers go to work, attend to special obligations or take some well-deserved "me-time" (to refresh and recharge), they do so knowing their loved one is safe and respected at Chiara Home. When you join the "Friends of Chiara Home" you make a pledge to help support these very special, loving families
